The firmware update page guides you through putting Mighty into update mode,
selecting the USB device, flashing the firmware, and restarting the device.

## Update from the browser

1. Plug Mighty Camera into your machine.
2. Open Chrome, Chromium, or Edge.
3. Go to [mightycamera.com/update](https://mightycamera.com/update).
4. Wait for the page to find Mighty and prepare the update.
5. Click **Select Device** when the page asks for the Rockchip device, then
   choose Mighty from the browser's USB device picker.
6. Keep the device connected while the page flashes the firmware and restarts
   the device.
7. If the browser asks again, click **Select Device** a second time and choose
   the re-enumerated Rockchip device.
8. Wait for **Update complete**.

Do not unplug Mighty while the update is flashing or restarting.

## If the device is not detected

If the update page cannot find the device, put it into bootloader mode manually:

1. Unplug Mighty.
2. Press and hold the board button.
3. While holding the button, connect the USB cable.
4. Release the button.
5. Open the update page and click **Select Device** at the bottom.

The update should continue from the Rockchip bootloader path. This path requires
you to
[recalibrate your device](/docs/calibration) after the update completes.
